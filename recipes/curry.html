Curry Recipe

Description

Curry is an indian and thai dish that dates back over 2000 years. It is commonly served with rice or vegetables

The ingredients can be simple or complex. Each curry dish has its own unique flavor, and each recipe is special.

Ingredients

  • Curry Paste
  • Chicken
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Fish Sauce
  • Chicken or Vegetable Broth
  • Sugar

Preparation

  1. Put the curry paste in a pot with a dash of olive oil
  2. Stir in diced chicken and onions
  3. Cook on medium heat for ten minutes then add half gallon broth
  4. Add garlic, ginger, fish sauce, and sugar and simmer until thick
