Purple Drank

Description

Purple drank is an American beverage that is typically consumed in southern and midwest states. It is commonly served over ice.

The origins of purple drank are shrouded in secrecy, but it is a highly popular beverage in certain US demographics.

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Water
  • Purple

Preparation

  1. Put cold water in a glass
  2. Add sugar and purple, stir untill well mixed
  3. Add ice and a straw for maximum enjoyment
