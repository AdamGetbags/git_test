Ahi Tuna

Description

Ahi tuna is a fish dish that can be eaten raw. It is commonly served over rice or with vegetables.

Ahi can be caught in the ocean and eaten immediately. It is recommended to chill the fish to preserve it.

Ingredients

  • Ahi Tuna Steaks
  • Garlic
  • Soy Sauce
  • Cucumbers
  • Honey
  • Black Pepper

Preparation

  1. Chop tuna into small cubes and dice garlic
  2. Add soy sauce to tuna and garlic to marinade
  3. Serve over sliced cucumbers drizzled with honey and ground black pepper
